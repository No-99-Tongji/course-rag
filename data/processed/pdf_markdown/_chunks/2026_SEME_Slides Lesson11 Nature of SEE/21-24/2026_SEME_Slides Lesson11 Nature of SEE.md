## 11.7 Benefits of learning SEE

## Benefits of learning SEE

Learning how to make good choices is the biggest benefit that you’ll realize from learning economics.

Most decisions are guided by the logic of costs and benefits. Accordingly, you can use positive economic analysis to predict other people’s behavior. Economics illuminates and clarifies human behavior.

Once you realize that you are constantly making economic choices, you’ll understand that the learning of this course is only your first step. You’ll discover the important cases or applications out of class.

The methods and tools of economics will improve your thinking and performance in all kinds of situations —making you a better engineer, a better consumer, and a better citizen.

Keep your eyes open and remember that every choice is economics in action.
